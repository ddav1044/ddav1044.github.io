Playstation/XBOX

Playstation

Common Problems and Fixes

    Playstation Not Turning On

  • Most likely due to faulty power supply

    • No Video Output

  • Typically an issue with HDMI port

    • Overheating

  • Most likely caused by dust build up on fan
  • Cleaning fans can help
  • Placing somewhere where it can get good airflow can also help

    • Disk Reading Error

  • Could be damage disk, try others
  • Reboot Playstation
  • Replace Optical Drive

*If your console needs parts replaced we are capable of ordering and replacing the part for you and will be included in price*
