Gaming Computers

Important Notice

Computers are a little more complex than gaming consoles. There are many things that have to be correct in order for the computer to run properly. If you are having issues please contact us and talk with us before you start attempting to repair if you do it on your own. Our experts can help trouble shoot your issues and get your computer running properly.

Common Problems

  • Software needing updates
  • Dirt or dust build up in computer
  • It sounds simple but making sure everything is connected properly (power supply, mouse and keybourd, video inputs, etc.)
  • Internet connection
  • Any type of damage to computer or hardware (most common would be water or electrical damage)

*If your computer needs parts replaced we are capable of ordering and replacing the part for you and will be included in price*
